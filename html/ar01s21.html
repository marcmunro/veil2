21. Contact Links

21.1. Reporting Bugs

If you find something that looks like a bug, please report it using the github issue tracker.

21.2. Contributing

All sorts of contibutions are welcomed. In particular:

  • documentation fixes and enhancements;
  • code fixes;
  • authentication functions;
  • translations.

If you'd like to get involved, you should chat with Marc using IRC or email (sorry that's not a very helpful link).

Oh, and feel free to fork the project if you get no response.

21.3. IRC

You may be able to find Marc on IRC at freenode.net. If possible he will be in the #veil2 room with the nick dbdude.

Feel free to drop in for a chat. You may be able to get advice and help. Marc is usually pretty friendly. His home timezone is PST.

21.4. Email

Marc is not going to publish his email address here as he gets enough spam as it is. But he isn't that hard to find with a bit of googling. Put Veil2 in the email subject line and you're less likly to fall into the spam bucket. If all else fails, raise an issue and he'll probably get back to you.

21.5. Availability

Marc is not paid for this and likes to believe he has a life. He may not be available for extended periods of time, so please be patient.
